About me

I am a fourth year Ph.D student in the School of Computer Science at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Zuxuan Wu and Prof. Yu-Gang Jiang.
Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree of Computer Science at Tongji University in 2020.

I am interested in computer vision and deep learning. My current research focuses on large multimodal models (LMMs).
